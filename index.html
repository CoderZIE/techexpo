PAST EXHIBITORS
Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO)

Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), globally acknowledged as one of the foremost institutions in space exploration and research, has made endlessly extravagant achievements one after the other and aims to continue on its path of glory. The amazing gift of cutting-edge technology that ISRO possesses aims to uncover the countless mysteries of the vast unknown.

Reliance

Reliance Industries is India's largest private sector company on all major financial parameters. Its diversified industries include textiles, petrochemicals, natural gas, retail, telecommunications, and energy. Apart from being a member of the Fortune 500, Reliance Industries Limited is also ranked 54th by Forbes Global 2000 and was named one of India's Most Sustainable Companies for 2021–2022, according to BW Businessworld.

Playing Station VR

Have you experienced the wonders of the virtual world? VRC (The Virtual Reality Company) is a content studio and production company that combines the best in technology, art and gaming to create compelling and immersive VR experiences for people around the world. Watching a living, breathing world streaming all around you with a seamless field of view is incredibly fascinating.

Audi

With their cutting-edge technology and their fantastic body designs, Audi cars are simply too amazing to pass up. The corporation has been extraordinarily successful in recent years in establishing a stronger foothold in the nation's auto market due to its exceptional models and stunning creative design. Out of all the versions it sells in the country, Audi's SUV portfolio is particularly well-liked by the general public and is the company's primary area of focus.

Ultratech

UltraTech, being Aditya Birla Group’s cement flagship company, is the third-largest manufacturer of cement in the world and the largest in India. Its building products have been strengthening the houses of countless people across the globe. This initiative aims to work with home builders at all stages of the building process, equip them with high-quality building materials and services, and help them finish their dream homes. With more than 3000 stores across India, UltraTech Building Solutions is currently the largest single-retail chain in the domains of construction and housing.
